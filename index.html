About

My name is Daiana, and I have over 10 years of experience in graphic, visual, and motion design, as well as creative direction. I bring a unique blend of creative vision, technical expertise, strategic thinking, and strong organizational skills to every project.

I began my career in Argentina after earning a Bachelor's Degree in Graphic Design. I worked at a film production company alongside motion graphics designers and VFX artists, and later led a design team in a corporate environment. I then transitioned into freelancing, collaborating with a creative agency and a video production company. During this time, I specialized in explainer videos for high-tech clients, translating complex ideas into compelling, easy-to-understand animations.

Now based in the Bay Area and working as a Motion Graphics Designer, I produce a wide range of animated content for both Marketing and Product teams, optimize production workflows, and mentor fellow designers.

I'm a curious problem-solver, passionate about storytelling, motion, and innovation. I continually explore new techniques and push creative boundaries. In recent years, I've been studying and exploring ways to integrate motion into the digital space, creating interactive products that bring designs to life and enhance user experiences.
